3x on dining, 2x on travel, no foreign transaction fee, strong travel insurance. Use it for every restaurant and museum.

Best simple
Capital One Venture / Venture X

2x on everything, no foreign fee, no categories to track. Venture X adds airport lounge access at CDG.

Best for ATMs
Charles Schwab debit

Reimburses every ATM fee worldwide. If you have it, use it for cash. Withdraw from bank ATMs only (BNP, Société Générale).

Never do thisSkip airport & hotel exchange booths (8–12% worse). Always choose to pay in euros, never dollars, when a card machine asks. Never use a credit card at an ATM — that's a cash advance at 20%+.

Cash to carry

€150–200 total for the week — markets, wine bars, tips, and ticket kiosks that want chip-and-PIN. Everything else goes on the card. Cards work almost everywhere.

Stay easy, stay aware

Safety

Paris is a safe city. Petty theft is the only real concern — and it's entirely preventable.

The honest baselineViolent crime against tourists is very rare. The risk is pickpocketing — real, but avoidable with basic awareness. You're far more likely to lose a phone than to be in any danger.

✗ Not on your itinerary — skip
    Gare du Nord at night · Barbès · Stalingrad · Porte de la Chapelle · Pigalle after midnight
Anti-pickpocket, in five lines
  • Bag crossbody, zipped, worn in front in any crowd.
  • Phone in the bag — never on a café table or in a back pocket.
  • The move is always distraction, then theft — a bump, a question, a petition. Keep walking.
  • Ignore the clipboard petition, gold ring, and friendship-bracelet scams entirely.
  • Leave passports & extra cash at the hotel; carry a photo of your passport.

Bookings

In priority order. Tap a link to book. Museum slots for July go 2–4 weeks out.

Smartest first buyThe Paris Museum Pass (4-day, €105pp) covers the Louvre, Orsay, Orangerie, Sainte-Chapelle & Versailles — and lets you skip the ticket-buying queue at each. Still book a free timed slot at every museum separately.

Roughly, pre-booked (3 people)

Passes €315 · restaurants ~€1,600 · food tour ~€350 · cruise €51 · airport cab ~€70 = about €2,400. Budget €150–200 pp per day on top for food, wine, shopping and cabs.
